International Women’s Day

08 Mar 2022

The A4 team and the Road Map team are proud to support International Women’s Day 2022.

Our group is 50% female, each with diverse backgrounds and a variety of interests including ocean sciences, coding, modelling and statistics. Below, we present the range of different research topics that inspire us to discover more in these areas and outline the paths that we have taken into the field.

  • Dr Niamh Cahill: Modelling Sea Level Change
  • Maeve Upton: Statistical Models for sea level change
  • Dr Samantha Hallam: It’s all about air-sea interaction
  • Catherine O'Beirne: Applications of Decadal Predictions
  • Dr Zoe Roseby: Historic Sea Level Change and Outreach
  • Ashly Uthaman: Predicting "Dancing Water"
  • Dr Levke Caesar: The AMOC and Climate Change
